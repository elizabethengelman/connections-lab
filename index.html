Summer Session Reflections

Documenting the process of creating, including the challenges, questions and ideas that came from that process was one of my favorite things about the summer session. Discovering that the process can be the work itself felt familiar to me, as I love to capture the small, intricate pieces in life. Yet structuring this process of capture in a blog form felt new and novel. I am excited about the end result from that time, and am excited to continue to add to this collection. The following includes a recap blog post for each week of the summer session, each including the links to all of the documentation I generated during that week.
